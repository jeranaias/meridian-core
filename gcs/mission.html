COMPASS CALIBRATION

Why calibrate?

Boat hulls, motors, cables, and electronics corrupt magnetic heading. Calibration maps those distortions so the EKF's 4-source heading fusion (mag + IMU + GPS COG + gyro) gets a clean magnetometer input at rest.

Safety: Vessel must be in water, far from other metal structures, engine off.

What to do:

  1. Press START below
  2. Slowly rotate the boat through all 6 axes over 60-90 seconds:
    • Full 360° turn to port, then to starboard
    • Nose up / nose down (pitch)
    • Rock left / right (roll)
  3. Keep turning until the coverage indicator shows ≥80% in all axes
  4. Press ACCEPT to save
